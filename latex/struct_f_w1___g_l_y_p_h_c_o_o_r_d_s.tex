\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Coordinates for a single glyph in the atlas. 

Each glyph image inserted in a glyph sheet texture gets a unique index in that sheet, and a corresponding \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_f_w1___g_l_y_p_h_c_o_o_r_d_s}{F\+W1\+\_\+\+G\+L\+Y\+P\+H\+C\+O\+O\+R\+DS}} entry in the sheet\textquotesingle{}s coord buffer, describing its location in the sheet as well as its dimensions.
