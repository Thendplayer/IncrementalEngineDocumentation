\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A vertex corresponding to a single glyph. 

When an I\+F\+W1\+Text\+Renderer draws a string, each output glyph is converted to an \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_f_w1___g_l_y_p_h_v_e_r_t_e_x}{F\+W1\+\_\+\+G\+L\+Y\+P\+H\+V\+E\+R\+T\+EX}} entry in an I\+F\+W1\+Text\+Geometry object.
