Detailed Description

Coordinates for a single glyph in the atlas.

Each glyph image inserted in a glyph sheet texture gets a unique index in that sheet, and a corresponding FW1_GLYPHCOORDS entry in the sheet's coord buffer, describing its location in the sheet as well as its dimensions.
